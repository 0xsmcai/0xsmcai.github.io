SMC FACTORY POSITIONING

AI agents that don't make money are toys.

SMC Factory is an autonomous startup factory built on top of GStack skills for Claude Code. It discovers, validates, and launches startups without human involvement.

SMC does not build products. It builds a system that repeatedly turns ideas into revenue generating businesses in the shortest possible time.

Each startup is a unit of output. The factory compounds. More runs produce more businesses, more data, and better decisions.

Revenue is not centralized. It is distributed by design across participants in the system.

SMC improves itself with every cycle. It publishes what it builds, how it performs, and how revenue flows.
